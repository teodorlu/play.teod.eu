As a tech person, what might one learn?

I find that to be a hard question. Our industry is moving. Our tools are changing. And as we get better, we gain agility. We don’t slow down, we speed up.

So I’m hesitant to leave this document as the answer for forever. I’ll strive for lindy compatibility where possible, but not at the expense of clarity.
