I often get to spend some time reading in my easter holiday (after a daily dose of cross country skiing, as is the custom in Norway). I could have picked the next unread title on my Kindle. But … how do I know that title is worth reading?

I don’t trust the recommendations I get from Amazon. Those are similar to things I’ve read. But I don’t want to optimize for “looks the most like something I’ve read”. I want something good.

To solve for this, I asked for reading recommendations. And I got so much more than I asked for. So I figured I’d collect the responses, put them into tables, and give those tables an URL. You’re currently reading that URL!

I believe curation is valuable, and I believe curation should be done by humans. Matthias, Ben Sless, Mario Tross, Denis McCarthy and Jack Rusher: thanks a lot for your recommendations! Also, this site would not have existed without Babashka (by Michiel Borkent), Pandoc (by John MacFarlane) and Org-roam (by Jethro Kuan), thank you!

Without further ado, here are the recommendations:
